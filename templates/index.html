Control Your Cursor
With Your Eyes

EyeCursor uses advanced eye-tracking technology to control the mouse cursor through eye movements. Fast, accurate, hands-free computing experience.

Features

High Accuracy

Precise eye tracking for smooth cursor movement.

Real-Time Tracking

Ultra-low latency eye detection and cursor control.

Blink Click

Blink your eye to perform mouse clicks naturally.

Privacy First

All processing remains on your local machine.

How It Works

1. Open Camera

Allow webcam access.

2. Eye Detection

MediaPipe tracks eye landmarks.

3. Cursor Movement

Eye movements control cursor direction.

4. Blink Click

Blink to perform left-click actions.

About EyeCursor

EyeCursor is an AI-powered accessibility project built using OpenCV, MediaPipe and PyAutoGUI to help users control computers through eye movements.
